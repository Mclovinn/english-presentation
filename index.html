What is Enduro?

Enduro is a form of motorcycle sport run on courses that are mostly off-road. For example in the mountain with a lot of obstacles like a rocks, tree roots, rivers, etc.

Different Types

  • Off-Road: like the example that I already presented to you. This is generally practiced like a hobby.
  • Outdoor: The circuit is made off-road and in this modality the time of the lap is controlled and the racer with minor time wins
  • Indoor: Is a race with obstacles.
